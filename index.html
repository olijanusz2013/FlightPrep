FlightPrep

FlightPrep is a website aiming to help you succeed in msfs 2020/24 and Vatsim

MSFS 2024 Tips

  1. Download free mods and add ons to msfs from Flightsim.to
  2. Buy premium products only, don't spend money on something that isnt good quality
  3. If you like a certain aircraft manufacturer only buy there planes, dont buy a diffrent company plane, you most likly won't fly it!
  4. If you want to practice your atc skills and dont want to fly on vatsim, use beyond atc or say intentions

VATSIM Tips

  1. Don't worry, you'll be fine!
  2. If you want to practice your atc skills and dont want to fly on vatsim, use beyond atc or say intentions
  3. Hop onto observer mode in Vatsim and learn your way through
  4. Have a notepad, or something, to write atc commands on
  5. If you don't know, just ask. The controllers are here to help!

Extra Advice

  1. Dont fly with a mouse, What is wrong with you?! Get like the thrustmaster sidestick or something, its good quality for the price!
  2. Join forums, group chats, there all here to help you
